ILLUSTRATION

FOOD STORY
Vietnamese gastronomy is diverse. These 2 dishes illustrated on 2 different drawings are actually two of the most famous Vietnamese street food. The first drawing’s dish is called “Khuc Bach sweet gruel” and the other is Egg coffee. Not only does the drawings promote the diversity of Vietnamese street food but they also represent two seasons: the summer whose the Khuc Bach sweet gruel is most consumed and the winter (whose egg coffee becomes a vital part of every Vietnamese breakfast)

Yoga Postures
The core of this work of art represents 2 ideologies: feminism and racism. The idea of feminism refers to the armpit of each alphabet character, which implies the double standard nowadays that only men can only men can have underarm hair. The racial ideology is found in the dress’s color whose main character wears. this is to be concerned about a racial myth that black women cannot wear pink outfits, inspired by a journal article. This cute and meaningful alphabet could be applied on advertising yoga class or kindergarten
